How Does This Work?

Understanding our AI credit scoring system

01. What Data We Use

Traditional banks look at your credit card history and past loans. But what if you've never had those? We look at other things that show you're reliable:

  • Income & Employment: How long you've been working and your income level
  • Assets: Whether you own a car or property
  • Family Size: Number of dependents (helps understand financial responsibility)
  • Employment Type: Self-employed, salaried, daily wage worker, etc.

02. What We DON'T Use

To keep things fair, we never look at:

  • Gender or age
  • Religion or caste
  • Your exact address or neighborhood
  • Social media activity

These things shouldn't determine if you can get a loan.

03. Built Responsibly

This is just a tool to help - not the final decision. High-risk cases are always reviewed by a real person. And we only use your data if you give us permission.

Note: This is a demonstration project using the Home Credit Default Risk dataset for educational purposes.
